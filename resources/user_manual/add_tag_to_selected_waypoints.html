Add tag to selected waypoints

After selecting a waypoint or multiple waypoints (see FAQ) and pressing this button, a label is added to the point.

You can choose from the following predefined tags:

  • FLYOVER
  • FLYBY
  • RH180 – Right Hand 180°
  • RH270 – Right Hand 270°
  • LH180 – Left Hand 180°
  • LH270 – Left Hand 270°

Additionally,  you can create a custom tag.  Please ensure that the custom tag adheres to the following restrictions; otherwise, it will not be added.

Restrictions for custom tags:

  • May only consist of capital letters (upper case),  whitespaces,  and the backslash (/) character.
  • Must not exceed 10 characters.

Viewing Tags:

After adding a tag, select the corresponding waypoint to see the tag displayed in the status bar at the bottom of the screen.

To view all waypoints and their associated tags:

  1. Select the waypoint layer.
  2. Open the Attribute Table (right-click → “Open Attribute Table”).
